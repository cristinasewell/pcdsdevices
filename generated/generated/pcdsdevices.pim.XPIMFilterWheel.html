pcdsdevices.pim.XPIMFilterWheel¶

class pcdsdevices.pim.XPIMFilterWheel(prefix, *, name, **kwargs)¶

Controllable optical filters to prevent camera saturation.

There are six filter slots, five with filters of varying optical densities and one that is empty. The enum strings here are T100, T50, etc. which represent the transmission percentage of the associated filter.
